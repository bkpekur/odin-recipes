Tuozaafi

Description

Tuozaafi is prepared from dried maize or sorghum flour.The flour is mixed with water and boiled over fire for 7 to 10 mins. Some of the dried flour is then continuosly added to the boiling mixture whilst stirring. When the desired level of thickness is achievded addition of the dried flour is stopped. Some of the boiled mixture is set and added back to the thick paste to make it soft. The thick paste is then moulded into desired shapes and served with green-leafy soup

Ingredients

For Tuozaafi:

  • Dried corn/sorghum flour
  • Lemon
  • Clean Water

For Soup:

  • Ayoyo or gboma leaves
  • Fresh Onions
  • Garlic
  • Smoked Fish
  • seasoning
  • cow meat/goat meat/chicken(as preferred)

Steps

  1. Pour a litreof clean water into a cooking pot and bring to the boil
  2. Mix 2 parts of water and 1 part of flour in a mixing bowl
  3. Mix the water and flour into a uniform solution and pour into the pot of water
  4. Stir gently and let it cook for 2-5mins
  5. Fetch 3 parts of this mixture and set asise
  6. Gently add the dried flour into the remaing liquid in the pot while stirring
  7. When the mixture becomes thick stop adding the flour but keep stirring to avoid lumps
  8. Gently add back the mixture set aside after 2-3mins of stirring
  9. Keep stirring until a uniform and smooth mixture is obtained
  10. This mixture can be moulded into desired sahpes and served
