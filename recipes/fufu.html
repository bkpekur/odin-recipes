Fufu

Description

Fufu is prepared by pounding boiled cassava(some prefer to add boiled plantain as well) into soft and thick paste. This paste is then slowly turned and moulded into the desired sizes which is enjoyed with light-soup, groundnut soup or "ebunubunu"-local parlance for green soup prepared using snails.

Ingredients

For Fufu:

  • Fresh Cassava
  • Fresh Plantain
  • Clean Water

For Soup:

  • Garden Egg
  • Fresh Onions
  • Garlic
  • Smoked Fish
  • seasoning
  • cow meat/goat meat/chicken(as preferred)

Steps

  1. Peel and wash the cassava and the plantain
  2. Cut the cassava and plantian into sizeable chunks
  3. Add enough water to almost cover the plantain and cassava in the pot
  4. Bring the pot and its content to a boil
  5. Occassionally check and add water until the chunks are soft
  6. Drain the water and allow to cool
  7. Pound the chunks and turn as needed until a smooth paste is obtained
  8. Add sprinkles of water as you pound and turn
  9. When the mixture becomes smooth in texture,cut and mould into desired shapes and serve
