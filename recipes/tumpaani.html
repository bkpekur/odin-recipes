Tumpaani

Description

Tumpaani is prepared by boiling beans flour which has been moulded into puddings These pudding are wrapped in leaves and boiled in hot water for about 30 minutes after which they are served with stew or pepper fried briefly in hot oil.

Ingredients

For Tumpaani:

  • Dry Beans Flour
  • Clean Water
  • Washed wrapping leaves

For Stew:

  • Vegetable Cooking Oil
  • Fresh Onions
  • Powdered/freshly blended pepper/li>

Steps

  1. Fetch 3 parts beans flour and 1 part water into a mixing bowl
  2. Mix well into a thick smooth paste
  3. Cut the thick paste and use the leaves to completely wrap around
  4. Bring a pot of water to the boil
  5. Carefully transfer the wrapped puddings into the pot of boiling water
  6. Boil for 30mins, occassionally adding water as needed
  7. Take it off the fire and drain off any residual water
  8. Allow to cool, unwrap and serve
